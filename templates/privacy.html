Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data when you use our personal expense tracking application.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you register, as well as the expense records, categories, amounts, and dates you log while using the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide the core features of Spendly — logging expenses, generating summaries, and showing spending patterns. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely in our database. We take reasonable technical measures to protect this information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party services (such as hosting or font providers) to operate the app. These providers only process the minimum data necessary to deliver their service and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact details provided on our website.
